## Topics Discussed

- We opened the meeting with introductions from members across various working groups.
- The group walked through the charter document to align on our mission of building a shared taxonomy standard and ecosystem directory.
- We outlined our primary goals of eliminating siloed terminology and preventing duplicate research across the foundation.
- Junjie demonstrated the new decoupled data and web portal system for managing the taxonomy and landscape map.
- The team reviewed the proposed SKOS Lite metadata schema for taxonomy and the CNCF-style structure for landscape mapping.
- We discussed the PR-driven contribution process where any member can propose changes while domain editors manage approvals.
- The group explored the mechanics of resolving cross-workgroup conflicts and establishing clear acceptance criteria for new terms.
- We aligned on our meeting cadence and opted for a weekly sync on Mondays starting in June.

## Decisions

- We will rely on a PR-driven, asynchronous workflow for managing all taxonomy contributions.
- We will utilize the SKOS Lite schema for taxonomy data and a CNCF-style structure for our ecosystem landscape.
- We will implement a formal review process requiring approval from designated domain editors to resolve terminology conflicts.
- We established a weekly meeting cadence on Mondays to maintain momentum as the workstream ramps up.
